My journey in code begins...

After being made redundant from my position as an Account Manager at FIS in 2020, where I had worked continuously for almost 10 years, I decided to take some time out from working to consider my next career move. After looking at what options were available to me and potential new career paths, I decided to embark on a new journey in software development via the Northcoders fullstack JavaScript bootcamp that was being offered for free via the UK Government re-skilling programme. As I worked through the learning materials required to pass the entry challenge for the course and then graduating from the course itself in April 2022, I have found coding to be a challenging yet extremely rewarding endeavour and one that I am excited to continue in a professional capacity. Although I have experience with both frontend and backend development, it's in building websites and frontend applications that I especially find most enjoyable and inspiring!

Since graduating from the Northcoders bootcamp I have continued to work towards my goal of becoming a developer and continue to practise and develop my skills and hope that this will result in me being able to gain my first professional role in the very near future!
